About Distribution Drives

Nyasa, in collaboration with the institute's social outreach, organized multiple distribution drives benefiting construction workers, metro workers, and contractual staff. We distributed clothing, bedding, footwear, masks, reusable sanitary napkins, mosquito repellent coils, fruit packets, and other essential items to hundreds of families.
