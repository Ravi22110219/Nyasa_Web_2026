About the Pathway Program

Nyasa has always been for the students, by the students. We started with the idea of bringing smiles to those who lost their smiles in the struggles of life. The Pathway Program aims to admit children studying in Nyasa school to regular schools, addressing the challenges faced by migrating workers in securing education for their children.

Program Implementation

01

Special Training Program (STP)

Conducted in collaboration with Basan Primary School in 2021-22. Twenty children participated, with 15 found eligible for the program. 12 children successfully completed the STP program.

02

Language Barrier Solution

Identified Gujarati medium teaching as a barrier for migrating workers' children. Basan Primary School issued transfer certificates to help children join Hindi/English medium schools.

03

School Partnership

Partnered with Colonel's Heritage School (English medium) at Chiloda. Annual fee of ₹14,000 per child includes uniforms, transportation, and books. Financial support provided through campus development.
