Cultural Celebration

Festival of Joy & Learning

Ganesh Chaturthi Celebration

Basan Kids joined the Ganesh Utsav celebrations at IITGN, enjoying various activities. They created portraits of Lord Ganesh, got inspired by the theme of Chandrayaan 2, and received stationery kits for participation.

Creative Activities Stationery Kits Chandrayaan 2 Theme

Nature Fest

Wildlife Conservation

Wildlife Week Celebration

Palaj government school children participated in National Wildlife Week events including documentary screenings, interactive quizzes, and wild species exhibitions. Nyasa organized the "Nature Fest" event with fantastic fancy dress show.

Documentary Screening Interactive Quizzes Wild Species Exhibition

Astronomy Session

Explore the Universe

Taare Zameen Par

Nyasa, in collaboration with Odyssey - The Astronomy Club, organized a star gazing session for the students of Basan Government School. Children observed Jupiter and the moon through telescopes and learned about the cosmos.

Telescope Viewing Astronomy Learning Odyssey Collaboration

Reading Session

Organized for students in grades 3-5 at Government Primary School Basan with guidance from Padma Shri awardee Sathyanarayan Mundayoor. Students enjoyed poetry recitals and animated storytelling.
