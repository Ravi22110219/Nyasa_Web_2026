About Sanjeevani

Sanjeevani, the flagship event of Nyasa, is a health camp that brings together people from villages, community workers, and doctors to a common point where they can interact, engage and resolve their health issues.

The 8th Sanjeevani health camp was held in Borij village, Gandhinagar, on February 4, 2024. Over 500 people from the community were helped with basic health checkups and immediate medication.
