Nyasa Summer Camp 2023

10 days of learning, fun, and growth for 100+ children

May 25 - June 3, 2023 IIT Gandhinagar Campus 100+ Children

Summer Camp Overview

Nyasa concluded a successful summer camp for 100+ kids from the nearby villages and IITGN construction worker colonies. This 10-day event during the summer break included activities such as sports, music, dancing, arts & crafts, entrepreneurship, archaeology, site visits, awareness sessions, and computer training.
